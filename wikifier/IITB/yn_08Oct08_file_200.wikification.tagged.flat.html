Hercules Technology Announces Portfolio Company , Teleflip , Inc., a Previously Graded 5 Investment, has Successfully Sold its Intellectual Assets Tuesday October 7, 4:05 pm ET Representing a $650,000 Recovery for Hercules PALO ALTO , Calif.--( BUSINESS WIRE )--Hercules Technology Growth Capital, Inc. (NASDAQ: - ), the leading specialty finance company providing venture debt and equity to venture capital and private equity backed technology and life science companies at all stages of development, today announced that it has received $650,000 in the form of common stock on the company s debt financing to Teleflip , Inc. Teleflip sold its assets to an undisclosed buyer in October 2008 . Teleflip was written down to a fair value of $0.00 in the first quarter of 2008. The sale represents a recovery of $650,000 or $0.02 per share in Net Asset Value . Teleflip is the second such repayment that Hercules has received in recent months and demonstrates our consistent ability to work with our portfolio companies in distressed situations in order to receive full repayments of our original loans, said Manuel A. Henriquez, co-founder, chairman and chief executive officer of Hercules. In August 2008 , Teleflip a mobile communications company delivering free e-mail service to any U.S. mobile phone announced to its subscribers that the company would shut down its services and explore selling its technology. An undisclosed buyer agreed to purchase Teleflip s intellectual assets for approximately $650,000 of its common stock. Hercules provided approximately $1 million in debt financing to Teleflip in June 2007 . About Hercules Technology Growth Capital , Inc.: Hercules Technology Growth Capital, Inc. is a NASDAQ traded specialty finance company providing debt and equity growth capital to technology and life science companies at all stages of development. Founded in December 2003 , the company primarily finances privately held companies backed by leading venture capital and private equity firms. Hercules invests in a broad range of ventures active in technology and life science industries and offers a full suite of growth capital products up and down the capital structure. The company is headquartered in Palo Alto , Calif. and has additional offices in the Boston , Boulder , San Diego and Chicago areas. Providing capital to publicly-traded or privately-held companies backed by leading venture capital and private equity firms involves a high degree of credit risk and may result in potential losses of capital.
