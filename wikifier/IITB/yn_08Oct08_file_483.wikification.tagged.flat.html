SideBar Related Items Videos Los Angeles -- Three men were shot, one fatally, in South Los Angeles , and their attacker remained at large Tuesday, police said. A motive for the triple shooting, which occurred just before midnight Monday in the vicinity of Figueroa and 75th streets, was unclear. A man approached the victims on foot and opened fire, said Los Angeles Police Department Officer Sam Park. Kevin Dion Baldwin, 33, of Los Angeles , was standing in a driveway on Figueroa when the gunfire rang out, said Assistant Coroner Chief Ed Winter. Of the two other men wounded, one was treated and released at the scene. A condition report on the other survivor was unavailable.
