Primary Navigation Secondary Navigation Search: Injured Diarra pulls out of France matches Mon Oct 6 , 3:21 PM ET PARIS (AFP) - Portsmouth midfielder Lassana Diarra has pulled out of France 's forthcoming matches with a left knee injury , it was revealed on Monday. Diarra will therefore neither play in France 's World Cup qualifier in Romania on October 11 nor four days later in a friendly against Tunisia at the Stade de France . Team officials said they would make an announcement on a possible replacement on Tuesday.
