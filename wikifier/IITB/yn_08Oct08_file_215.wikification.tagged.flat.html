October 8 , 2008 Breaking News, Weather, Sports and Entertainment for Georgia and South Carolina Bond set for Orangeburg County man accused of killing teen while hunting Tools Story Published: Oct 6, 2008 at 4:59 PM EDT Story Updated: Oct 6, 2008 at 4:59 PM EDT ROWESVILLE , S.C . (AP) - Authorities say bond for an Orangeburg County man accused of killing a teenager while hunting in the woods was set at $250,000. Orangeburg County Sheriff 's spokesman Tom Brown said Dennis Hall faces a charge of involuntary manslaughter in the death of Joshua Gainey. Coroner Samuetta Marshall has said the 16-year-old died Thursday evening from a gunshot wound to the chest. Authorities said he was hunting with friends. WIS-TV reports that Hall was also hunting. Investigators told the station that Hall heard something in the bushes, shot, then ran back to his house. Gainey played football at Branchville High. Hall was still in jail on Thursday afternoon . It was unclear if he had a lawyer. (Copyright 2008 by The Associated Press . All Rights Reserved .)
