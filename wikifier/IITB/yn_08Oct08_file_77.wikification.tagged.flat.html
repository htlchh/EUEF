Web NASDAQ.com Page: Symbol List: | | | Crisis Talks In Bolivia End Without Agreement LA PAZ , Bolivia (AFP)--President Evo Morales and four autonomy-seeking regional governors failed to reach agreement Sunday after 10 hours of closed- door talks to find a solution Bolivia 's recent political upheaval, a government official said. Morales and the four regional leaders also failed to set a date to resume their dialogue, according to Bolivia 's minister for rural development Carlos Romero . The meeting Sunday - the third between the parties in eight months - took place in Cochabamba in the center of the country, in the presence of observers from the U.N. and regional organizations. Morales, Bolivia 's first indigenous president, has struggled to assert his authority over the wealthy eastern half of the country where rebel governors have fought for autonomy and against a new constitution. Dialogue froze between the two sides about eight months ago, and violence has flared again in recent weeks in the divided country, the poorest in South America , raising fears of civil war. Morales and governors from the regions of Santa Cruz , Beni , Tarija and Chuquisaca have received a report from technical working groups that have been trying since Sept. 18 to find a solution to the crisis.
