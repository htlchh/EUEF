DINING 12:30 p.m. ý 11th annual Pastries in Paradise to feature Halloween theme Pacific Daily News  life@guampdn.com  October 8, 2008 12:30 p.m., Oct. 8 ý The 11th annual Pastries in Paradise will be held next at the Hyatt Regency Guam hotel. This year's event will feature a Halloween theme with a dozen vendors participating. Also, there will be competitions, including the Bailey's dessert challenge, Torani beverage challenge, judges cup and people's Choice awards . Tickets are $30 each for those 12 years and older and $15 for children ages 6 to 11 and are available at the Pacific Daily News , the Hyatt Regency Guam hotel and the Guam Hotel and Restaurant Association.
