SOCCER- CANADA BEAT PANAMA 3-1 IN WORLD CUP QUALIFIER .
EDMONTON 1996-08-31
Canada beat Panama 3-1( halftime 2-0) in their CONCACAF semifinal phase qualifying match for the 1998 World Cup on Friday.
Scorers:
Canada- Aunger( 41st min, pen), Paul Peschisolido( 42nd), Carlo Corrazin( 87th)
Panama- Jorge Luis Dely Valdes( 50th)
Attendance: 9,402
