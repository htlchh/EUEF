RUGBY UNION- SOUTH AFRICA BEAT ALL BLACKS 32-22.
JOHANNESBURG 1996-08-31
South Africa beat New Zealand 32-22( haltime 16-8) in the final test match of their three-test series at Ellis Park on Saturday.
Scorers:
South Africa- Tries: Joost van der Westhuizen ( 2), Andre Joubert .
Conversion: Henry Honiball .
Penalties: Honiball( 2), Joubert ( 3).
New Zealand- Tries: Sean Fitzpatrick , Walter Little, Justin Marshall .
Conversions: Andrew Mehrtens ( 2).
Penalties: Mehrtens.
New Zealand win test series 2-1.
