Lebed , Chechens start peace talks.
KHASAVYURT, Russia 1996-08-30
Russian peacemaker Alexander Lebed and Chechen separatist military leader Aslan Maskhadov started a new round of peace talks on Friday in this settlement just outside the rebel region.
Lebed , who flew into Chechnya earlier in the day, said he hoped to sign a framework agreement on a political settlement of the 20-month conflict in which tens of thousands of people have died.
Neither Lebed nor Maskhadov made any statement before the talks.
