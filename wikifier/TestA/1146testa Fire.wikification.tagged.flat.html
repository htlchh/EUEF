Fire destroys restaurant in Bahraini village.
MANAMA 1996-08-31
A fire has completely gutted a Turkish-operated restaurant in a Bahraini village, residents said.
They said a fire broke out at Shul'ala restaurant in the early hours on Saturday in al-Daih village, five km( three miles) west of the capital Manama .
It was not immediately clear what caused the fire or if there were any casualties.
Government officials had no immediate comment.
