Ericsson says wins 1.2 bln SKR China order.
STOCKHOLM 1996-08-30
Swedish telecoms group LM Ericsson AB said on Friday it won an order worth 1.2 billion crowns for a fixed public telecoms network in the Guangdong province of China .
Ericsson said in a statement the order was from the Guangdong Post and Telecommunications Administration( GPTA).
The order included AXE switching equipment, ISDN equipment, Intelligent Network ( IN) products, broad-band multi-media communication network products, services and training, Ericsson spokesman Per Zetterquist told Reuters .
Deliveries are due to be completed by 1999, the company said.-- Stockholm newsroom+46-8-700 1017
