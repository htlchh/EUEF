SOCCER- SILVA'S`LOST PASSPORT' EXCUSE NOT ENOUGH FOR FIFA .
MADRID 1996-08-30
Spanish first division team Deportivo Coruna will be without key midfielder Mauro Silva for Saturday's game with Real Madrid after FIFA , soccer's world governing body, suspended the Brazilian for one game for missing his national side's European tour.
Silva excused his absence from Brazil's game against Russia , on Wednesday, and Saturday's match with the Netherlands by saying he had lost his passport.
But that did not prevent him from collecting the one-match suspension.
